Description

nbsp;                                     NPR College of Engineering and Technology (NPRCET)is a co-educational institution established in 2008 by the Titan Educational Trust to provide technical education, particularly for rural students. Located near Natham in Tamil Nadu's Dindigul district, it became an autonomous institution in 2023 and is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University.The college offers a range of engineering and management programs and has received an A grade from NAAC.

About the college

  1. Establishment:

                            Founded in 2008 with the aim of uplifting students from rural areas through quality education.

  2. Location:

                            Situatedon a 100-acre campus in a pollution-free environment, located between the villages of Uluppakudi and Gopalpatti, at the foothills of Sirumalai.
